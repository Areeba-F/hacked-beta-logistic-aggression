How Were the Results Calculated?

The results from this detection were generated based on a logistic regression machine learning algorithim. This was done by analysizing previously collected data regarding patients age, gender, height, weight, systolic and diastolic blood pressure, cholesterol levels, glucose levels, smoking rate, alcohol intact, physical activity, and finally, whether or not they had caridovascular disease. Based on the results, the algorthim then learned how to predict such cases with a roughly 70% accuracy rate.

As this is the case, please contact a medical professional for further information.
